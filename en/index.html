Kamgrad is Croatia’s leading construction company

We have specialised in high-rise construction, and with continuous organic growth and positive business from our establishment to the present, we have strengthened our leading position.

40 years of experience and knowledge of our 750 employees have been incorporated into all our business and housing projects, and each new project is approached professionally – respecting the agreed deadlines, using state-of-the-art materials and state-of-the-art technological achievements.

Our quality construction and long-term reliability have been recognized not only on the Croatian market, but also on German, Swedish and Serbian markets. So far, we have successfully and in time of the agreed deadlines realized over 400 projects, the most important of which are the construction of the Zagreb, Split and Dubrovnik airports, the construction of the Park Hotel in Rovinj and Poreč, the construction of the Valamar Isabella resort and the Matrix Office Park in Zagreb and the reconstruction of the rotor in Zagreb.
